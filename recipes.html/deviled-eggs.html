Ingredients

  • eggs
  • vinegar
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. Add water to pot.
  2. Boil eggs.
  3. Separate yolks from eggs.
  4. Mix vinegar, mayonaise, and yolks together.
  5. Scoop yolks back into egg whites.
